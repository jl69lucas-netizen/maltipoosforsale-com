## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: US families (mobile-first, 79% mobile traffic) researching Maltipoo/Maltese puppies
- **Context**: Emotional purchase decision — searching from phones, often comparing multiple breeders
- **Job to be done**: Verify breeder is ethical/trustworthy, assess puppy fit for their lifestyle, inquire/apply
- **Emotional state**: Excited + cautious. They want warmth AND professional credibility simultaneously

### Brand Personality
- **3 words**: Warm · Ethical · Authoritative
- **Voice**: Expert breeder who is also a caring family — not a pet store, not a clinical operation
- **Emotional goals**: Trust (10-year guarantee, 20+ years), delight (puppy joy), urgency (puppies go fast)
- **Anti-references**: Cold clinical vet sites, cheap puppy mill aesthetics, over-designed luxury brands

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Light mode** (required — daytime browsing, family-friendly)
- **Orange-led**: Primary orange hero sections, white/off-white content zones, alternating backgrounds
- **Mobile-first**: 79% mobile traffic — all layouts must be thumb-friendly, tap targets ≥ 44px
- **High contrast**: Black text on white/surface (#000 on #F8F9FA = 16.8:1 ratio) ✓ WCAG AAA
- **Section rhythm**: Alternate white and #F8F9FA sections every 2-3 sections

### Design Principles
1. **Trust over flash** — Every design element should reinforce credibility (10-year warranty, 20+ years)
2. **Mobile thumb first** — CTAs at top AND bottom; no content buried below the fold
3. **Orange for action** — Use primary orange ONLY for CTAs and section highlights; never for body text
4. **Content density for SEO** — Long-form pages should be visually scannable with clear H2 anchors
5. **One clear CTA per section** — Never stack more than 2 buttons; hierarchy: primary (orange) > outline
