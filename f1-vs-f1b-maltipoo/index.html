Section 22: Training & Care Tips — Managing the Curl

The “Line Brushing” Technique

If you choose an F1b Maltipoo, you cannot just brush the top layer of fur. This is the #1 mistake new owners make. The top looks fluffy, but a solid mat forms underneath against the skin. You must learn Line Brushing—parting the hair to the skin and brushing out small sections at a time.

  2. Frequency: F1b = Daily. F1 = 3x/week.
  3. Professional Schedule: F1b = Every 4-6 weeks. F1 = Every 6-8 weeks.
