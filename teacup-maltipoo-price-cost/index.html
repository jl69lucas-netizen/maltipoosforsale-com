Section 6: The “Hidden Truth” — The High Cost of a Cheap Puppy

Why a $600 Puppy Could Cost You $5,000

This is the most critical financial lesson in dog ownership: The purchase price is just the down payment. When you find a “Teacup Maltipoo” listed for $600 or $800, you aren’t finding a deal; you are likely finding a puppy mill victim. These dogs are often raised in filth, unvaccinated, and separated from their mothers too early.
